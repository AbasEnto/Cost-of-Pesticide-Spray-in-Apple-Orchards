Basic Assumptions

  • Spray costs consist of two components: insecticide material cost and spray application cost (fixed + variable).
  • Spray volume per kanal is taken as 60 L for 1–5 yr old plants, 100 L for 5–10 yr old plants, 160 L for 10–20 yr old plants, and 300 L for >20 yr old plants in case of traditional orchards.
  • In medium-density orchards, spray volume per kanal is taken as 111 L for 1–2 yr old plants, 222 L for 2–5 yr old plants, and 444 L for >5 yr old plants.
  • In high-density orchards, spray volume per kanal is taken as 167 L for 1–2 yr old plants, 250 L for 2–5 yr old plants, and 418 L for >5 yr old plants.
  • Insecticide dosages follow label, CIB&RC, and SKUAST-K advisories; costs based on lowest bulk market prices as of June 1, 2024.
  • Use of a company or product name does not imply approval or recommendation of the product to the exclusion of others that also may be suitable.
  • Power sprayer specifications: 6.5 HP petrol engine, 200 ft pipe, 10-year life, ₹35,000 purchase price, ₹33,000 current list price, average use of 10 sprays per season, 10 kanal orchard and 100 hrs annual use.
  • Depreciation calculated by ASA&BE and Straight-Line methods; salvage value 10% (Straight-Line) or 50% at 5 years (ASA&BE).
  • Interest rate: 8% (real) derived from 13% nominal minus 5% inflation.
  • TIH: 3% of average machine value annually (1% tax, 0.5% insurance, 1.5% housing).
  • Variable costs include repairs (10% of purchase price per year), fuel, lubrication, and labour.
  • Fuel consumption: 1.48 L/hr (petrol @ ₹100.31/L); lubrication: 15% of fuel cost.
  • Labour: Two workers @ ₹700/day each, adjusted +20% for non-field time.
  • Spray efficiency: Two labour units can spray 200 L/hr; cost = ₹210/hr total.
  • Orchards classified based on spray volume needed for labour requirement: Type I (<200 L/kanal) and Type II (200–450 L/kanal).
  • Equipment and water available near orchard; no rental or transport downtime included.
